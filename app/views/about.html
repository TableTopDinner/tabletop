What if there was a dining tool that allowed you to eat at your favorite restaurants at no extra cost while helping the charities you care about most? Welcome to TableTop.

 

Our Mission

Our main priority is to provide affordable, accessible and impactful philanthropic dining options for everyone who wants to help. Sometimes, giving to charity gets expensive. We're changing that by creating the first ever charitable dining platform where you - the individual - have the power to support organizations on your schedule at no additional cost. 

 

To be a part of the TableTop family, just follow 4 simple steps:

  1. Download the TableTop mobile application (coming soon)
  2. Register a credit card and choose a benefitting organization
  3. Search the interactive map for participating restaurants
  4. Dine out, just as usual

 

TableTop makes charitable dining easier than ever. No more coupons, no more premiums. Just make sure you're dining at a participating restaurant with a registered card. 

Benefits of using our platform

  • Help any non-profit by eating at your favorite restaurants.
  • Give more often without spending any more on dinner.
  • Discover great new restaurants and charities in your area.
  • Plus...
    • Restaurants will be filling their seats on slower nights, getting unique customers, and creating social impact.
    • Charities will have a new revenue source.
